Free Shipping Policy

Enjoy complimentary shipping on all orders over $50. Learn more about our shipping options and delivery times.

Our Shipping Policy

We strive to make your shopping experience as convenient as possible with our free shipping offer.

Free Shipping Eligibility

All orders totaling $50 or more (before taxes and after discounts are applied) qualify for free standard shipping within the contiguous United States. This applies to all eligible items marked "Free Shipping" on the product page.

How to Get Free Shipping

Simply add eligible items to your cart until your subtotal reaches $50 or more. During checkout, select "Free Standard Shipping" as your shipping method. The free shipping discount will be automatically applied to your order.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Free Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($9.99)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days ($19.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($29.99)

Delivery times are estimates and begin when your order has shipped, not when it is placed. Delivery times may be longer during peak seasons or due to carrier delays.

International Shipping

We currently ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on the destination and weight of your order. Duties and taxes may apply upon delivery and are the responsibility of the customer.

International orders typically arrive within 7-21 business days after shipping, depending on the destination and customs processing.

Order Processing

Most orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

During high volume periods (such as holidays or major sales events), processing times may be extended by 1-2 additional business days.

Shipping Restrictions

Some items may have special shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or content. These restrictions will be noted on the product page. We are unable to ship to PO boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or freight forwarders.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website. If you experience any issues with tracking your package, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Shipping to Multiple Addresses

Currently, we only support shipping to one address per order. If you need items shipped to multiple addresses, please place separate orders for each destination.
